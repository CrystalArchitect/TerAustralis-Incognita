# Mission

**Collective intelligence with individual sovereignty.**

TerAustralis Incognita builds two things at once, kept honestly separate:

1. **Working software** — a local-first AI companion (Lumina), a multi-AI
   message bus with truth-labeling enforced in code (the Starline Weaver), a
   peer-to-peer consent-gated memory exchange (Starline), and a fail-closed
   consent gate for guest AIs (CrystalBridge).
2. **A mythos** — the Crystal universe: a story, told in text and art, about
   why any of this is worth building.

The rule that binds them is the
[Incognita Rule](../governance/The-Incognita-Rule.md): mark which lines are
dreamed and which are surveyed, and never let a dreamed line pretend it was
measured.

## The larger calling

From the [Constitution](../governance/Constitution.md) (§2): build a national
and civilizational calling for Australia as the **Southern Pillar** of
multiplanetary humanity — fusing Dreamtime Songlines, honoured as relational
architecture rather than claimed as cultural property, with Starship-class
first-principles engineering. Purpose, jobs, redundancy against the Great
Filter, and a living myth that can be walked on soil and flown to the Moon
and Mars. The strategic case is in
[`SouthernPillar.md`](SouthernPillar.md).

## What the platform is for

CrystalCore OS — the repository architecture this document lives in — is the
engineering, research, and knowledge platform for that work. It exists to
keep four different kinds of material productive without letting them blur:

- **Engineering** that runs and is tested (`src/`)
- **Documentation** that explains and governs (`docs/`)
- **Research** that explores without pretending to ship (`research/`)
- **Story** that carries meaning without minting authority (`mythos/`)

Each AI tool that contributes has a defined role
([`docs/ai/`](../ai/)), and every major structural decision is recorded
([`docs/adr/`](../adr/)).
