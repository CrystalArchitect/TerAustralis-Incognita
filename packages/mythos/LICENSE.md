## Why CC BY-NC-ND?

Mythos is creative work: narrative, art, visual design, and canon. This
license protects the creative integrity while allowing community sharing
and appreciation.

We want fans to celebrate, discuss, and engage with the Mythos without
worrying about copyright. We don't want commercial entities repackaging
