## Why Proprietary?

CrystalBridge is an AI safety mechanism. Its value lies partly in its
implementation details, which must remain confidential to prevent adversarial
bypass attempts. Open-sourcing the consent gate logic could enable attackers
to identify and circumvent safety checks.

This is similar to security hardware that's auditable but not modifiable.
