# The Daily Digest — a real one

**What this is:** a prompt for making a short, honest digest of what *you actually
came across* — the links, posts, lyrics, or papers you paste in — rated for
resonance and boiled down to what's worth a closer look. It's the
[Signal Scanner](signal-scanner.md) run across a handful of real items at once.

**What this is not:** it does **not** invent signals, and it does **not** simulate
a "scan" of a day you didn't feed it. A version that generates fake posts and
assigns them fake 80/100 scores would be manufacturing a survey that never
happened — precisely the dreamed-as-surveyed move
[`../../docs/governance/The-Incognita-Rule.md`](../../docs/governance/The-Incognita-Rule.md) forbids. If you paste
nothing, there's nothing to report, and that's an honest answer. A quiet day is a
quiet day.

Vision-layer tool, like the rest of [`../tools/`](../tools/).

---

## The prompt

```
Help me make a short, honest digest of what I actually came across. I'll paste
real items — links, posts, lyrics, papers. Work only from what I give you: if you
can open a link, read it; if you can't, say so and use what I paste. Never invent
items, never add "signals" I didn't give you, and never pretend to have scanned
anything. If I paste nothing, tell me there's nothing to digest — don't fill the
space.

For each item I paste:
- a one-line plain summary
- Resonance: LOW / MEDIUM / HIGH — one honest line on why (resonance is not
  agreement; call a weak fit weak). Never a 0-100 number.

Then, across only the items I actually gave you:
- What stood out: the one or two most worth a closer look, and why.
- Anything worth sitting with, or replying to by hand (you draft nothing that
  gets auto-sent).

Keep it short. Don't dress a light day as a big one, and don't force a theme that
isn't there.
```

## Using it well

- Feed it your real day — a few links you genuinely saw. Its value is the honest
  sort, not the volume.
- The failure mode is the same as always: if it always finds the day "rich" and
  every item "high," it's flattering you, not reflecting. Trust the quiet reports.
- Want to act on something? Open the [scanner](signal-scanner.md) or the
  [master prompt](crystalcore-master.md) for a proper reflection, and write any
  reply yourself.

*Non Solus.*
